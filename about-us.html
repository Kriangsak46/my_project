Team & Rider Changes

Marc Márquez moved from Repsol Honda Team to Gresini Racing MotoGP, joining his brother Álex Márquez. Franco Morbidelli joined Prima Pramac Racing after leaving Monster Energy Yamaha MotoGP. Álex Rins transferred from LCR Honda Castrol to Monster Energy Yamaha MotoGP.

Playing MotoGP 2024: Experience the Speed and Thrill

MotoGP 2024 is the most realistic motorcycle racing game in the MotoGP series, featuring stunning graphics, improved physics, and intense racing modes. Players can compete against world-class riders, customize their bikes to match their riding style, and experience lifelike racing on legendary circuits.

The game offers various modes, including Career Mode, where you create and develop your own rider, Multiplayer Mode to challenge friends worldwide, and Time Trial Mode for perfecting lap times.

With realistic controls, players must master braking, cornering, and acceleration to gain an edge. The dynamic weather system adds an extra layer of challenge, making every race unpredictable and exciting.
